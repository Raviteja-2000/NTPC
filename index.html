About & Legal

This interactive guide is built for personal study. Previous-year question papers are generally protected by copyright. Please import only content that you created yourself, have permission to use, or is clearly licensed for reuse.

Where to legally access previous-year papers

  • Adda247 – RRB NTPC Previous Year Question Papers
  • SSCADDA – RRB NTPC Previous Year Question Papers
  • Testbook – RRB NTPC 2025 Question Papers (Memory-based)
  • Prepp – RRB NTPC Question Papers (links)
  • RRB Secunderabad (archived papers/guidance)

Once you download, you can convert those PDFs into CSV/JSON (respecting their terms) and import here for private study.
